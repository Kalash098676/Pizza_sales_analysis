# Pizza_Sales_Analysis

## 📌 Project Description

The Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence solution developed using Power BI to analyze pizza sales performance and customer ordering patterns. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights that help businesses understand revenue trends, customer preferences, product performance, and operational efficiency.

The project focuses on identifying key sales drivers, monitoring business KPIs, and uncovering opportunities for growth through data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance.
- Monitor revenue generation and order trends.
- Identify top-performing and low-performing pizzas.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate sales contribution by pizza category and size.
- Discover peak ordering days and hours.
- Support strategic business decisions through data visualization.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard tracks the following business metrics:

- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Pizzas Sold
- Average Order Value
- Average Pizzas Per Order
- Revenue Percentage by Category
- Revenue Percentage by Pizza Size

---

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis

### Sales Performance

The dashboard provides a complete overview of business performance by tracking total revenue, orders, and pizzas sold. Trend analysis helps identify growth patterns and seasonal fluctuations in sales.

### Customer Ordering Behavior

Customer ordering trends are analyzed based on:

- Orders by Day of Week
- Orders by Month
- Orders by Hour

These insights help identify peak business periods and optimize staffing and inventory planning.

### Product Performance

The dashboard evaluates pizza performance using:

- Revenue Generated
- Quantity Sold
- Total Orders

This analysis helps determine the most popular and profitable products.

### Category Analysis

Sales performance is segmented by pizza category:

- Classic
- Supreme
- Chicken
- Veggie

This enables comparison of category contribution toward total revenue.

### Size Analysis

The dashboard analyzes customer preferences across pizza sizes:

- Small (S)
- Medium (M)
- Large (L)
- X-Large (XL)
- XX-Large (XXL)

This helps understand demand distribution and inventory requirements.

## 💡 Key Insights

- Large-sized pizzas generate the highest revenue.
- Classic category pizzas contribute significantly to overall sales.
- Weekend sales are generally higher than weekday sales.
- Lunch and evening hours represent peak ordering periods.
- A small group of pizzas contributes a large portion of total revenue.
- Several products consistently underperform and may require promotional strategies.
